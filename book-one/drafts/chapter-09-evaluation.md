## Recommendation

### Elements to pull from other drafts:
1. **From Hearthkeeper:** Ryn's "match me" reframe — "pretend you're tired from work and you've got enough left for this one thing"
2. **From Lyricist:** Ryn's reversal speech — "the thing you're bad at is the thing I do every day. The normal amount. The regular human dose."
3. **From Lyricist:** The "hairline fracture — invisible, structural" image

### What NOT to import:

## Best Passages

**Carver — The gap measured in laughter:** "The laughter of two people who had just measured a gap and found it wider than either of them had imagined."

**Hearthkeeper — Match me reframe:** "Pretend you're tired from work and you've got enough left for this one thing and you need to be careful with it because there's dinner to heat and hands to warm and you can't afford to waste it."
