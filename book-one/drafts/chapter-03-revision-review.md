## New Issues Introduced

### ⏳ Pace Keeper

**1. Ash's conversation with Haran takes up a lot of space.** The evening conversation (lines 303–343) is long — Ash explains the Hall work in detail. This is mostly unchanged from the original and was not flagged before, but the tightening elsewhere makes this section feel comparatively heavy. **Not blocking** — the conversation earns its length through Haran's responses — but worth noting for future passes.

### 🔗 Continuity Tracker

**2. "Yesterday, Dorenne put me at the briefing table" (line 75).** In the revised Chapter 2, the briefing table invitation happens on Day Eight. If Chapter 3 takes place the evening of the last day of Week Two (Day ~14), "yesterday" would mean Day 13. This is a minor inconsistency — Ash had the table seat since Day 8, so he wouldn't describe it as "yesterday." **Recommend changing to:** "Dorenne put me at the briefing table" (drop "yesterday") or "Last week, Dorenne put me at the briefing table."

**3. "I was here three days ago" (line 157).** This is plausible but should be verified against the Chapter 2 timeline — if Ch. 2 ends on a specific day, Ch. 3 needs to be set 3+ days later for this to work. The two-week timeline gives enough room for this.

### ✍️ Voice Editor

**4. No issues.** Prose is consistent with the style guide. Third-person limited maintained throughout. No POV breaks. Emotion rendered physically. Dialogue naturalistic. Interior monologue properly italicized and sparse.

### 🧭 Arc Guardian

**5. No issues.** Ash's arc is on track: he's excited about Dorenne's world, can't see the value of what he already has, and the chapter correctly plants the irony through action (pump) and silence (Haran's non-response) rather than statement.

### 🌍 World Keeper

**6. No issues.** All magic usage is accurate. Haran's channeling is within parameters. Pool mechanics, the workshop's non-magical technology, and the district geography are consistent with `worldbuilding.md` and `magic-system.md`. The Loan tradition seed ("build what lasts longer than you do") is consistent with the tradition's philosophy without naming it.

### 🏗️ Story Architect

**7. Structure is improved.** The tightened tinkerer intros give Scene 3 better forward motion. The bridging object (deployment brief) creates a clean physical through-line. The walk-home trim removes redundancy without losing emotional weight.

### 💡 Thematic Compass

**8. Motif tracking: excellent.** Hands/tools, fire/warmth, open door, the two smiles, warmth-that-fades vs. warmth-that-sustains, ticking — all present and correctly deployed. The sensory echo (wall warmth → stove warmth) is a strong addition. The Loan tradition seed integrates naturally. The "build what lasts longer than you do" carries forward beautifully from the pump.
