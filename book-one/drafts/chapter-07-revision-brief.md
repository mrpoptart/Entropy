## Important Suggestions (8 — should address)

### 3. Closing Section Over-Explains
**Source:** Voice Editor, Story Architect, Arc Guardian, Thematic Compass
The final pages tell the reader what the chapter already showed. Specific cuts:
- **Lines 352–353:** "underneath the truth, something she would not say and he would not hear" — names the subtext, undermining it. Cut; end on "She said it the way she said things that were true."
- **Lines 372–373:** The Dorenne/Sorren comparison chain is over-constructed. Keep only the mother comparison: "the way his mother had fixed the cold… one pool at a time for twenty-three years."
- **Lines 101–102:** "his mother believed a growing boy needed more and a mother needed less" — end the sentence at "the last scraping always offered to Ash."

### 4. "Ambient Bleed" Arrives Unearned
**Source:** Pace Keeper
The maintenance worker's term "ambient bleed" (line ~245) is technical vocabulary that Ash hasn't been taught yet. Either have the worker explain it in-scene, or replace with a plain-language description Ash would use.

### 5. Maren Needs One More Beat of Visible Cost
**Source:** Story Architect, Arc Guardian, Thematic Compass
Maren's competence is well-rendered but her *depletion* is nearly invisible. Add one small physical tell — declining food, a longer pause before standing, cold hands — during one of her repair visits. Foreshadows Book Two decline without being heavy-handed.

### 6. Add One Beat of Dorenne Ledger Tension
**Source:** Arc Guardian
The chapter is entirely domestic, which is correct, but needs one small reminder that Ash's new life comes with strings. A brief mention — a note from Dorenne about scheduling, or Ash noticing the apartment's Commerce Bureau seal — would seed Thread 14.

### 7. Compress Scenes 3 + 4 (Salvage + New Gifts)
**Source:** Story Architect
These scenes cover similar emotional territory (old vs. new). Consider trimming ~300 words by merging the copper kettle purchase into the salvage scene rather than giving it a separate section break.

### 8. Strengthen One Neighborhood Interaction
**Source:** Evaluation (carried forward from Carver weakness)
The Vellen conversation works but the neighborhood section could use one more small exchange — even a greeting from another tenant — to texture the building's social silence.

### 9. Door Wedge — One More Beat
**Source:** Thematic Compass
The wedge moves from old apartment (line 125) to bedside shelf (line 365). Add one final touch: Ash's hand finding it on the shelf as he falls asleep, paralleling the mother touching the bowl. Lands without stating.

### 10. Father's Chisel — Sound Callback
**Source:** Thematic Compass
In the final section, when Ash sits alone, let him hear the old chisel from the back room. Connects the father's insistence on old tools to the mother's insistence on the old kettle — deepening the parallel between the parents' shared response to displacement.
